How accurate is your prediction model?

Our AI model achieves 95% accuracy in predicting employee attrition by analyzing 23 comprehensive factors including job satisfaction, work-life balance, performance ratings, and more.

How long does it take to get results?

Our system provides predictions within 2-3 seconds for individual employees. For batch analysis of multiple employees, results are typically available within 5-10 minutes.

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict data protection protocols. Your data is never shared with third parties and is stored securely in compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

Yes! We offer integrations with popular HR platforms like Workday, BambooHR, ADP, and others. We also provide API access for custom integrations.
